OUR LOVE STORY

Life without love is like a tree without
blossoms or fruit.

I have known Moses since I was a toddler, we grew up in the same branch church. We were in the same space every Sunday for 11 years. After my primary education, I gained admission into the same school he was at that time. He was my senior but we talk once in a while. I knew there was something special about him then, but I was too young to understand what it was.

One Monday afternoon in August 2016, he gave me a Bible as my birthday gift as requested. After that meeting, we both disappeared into thin air. We met after a practice in the church in May 2019, he offered to give my friends and I a ride to our school park. I called to thank him when we got home and that was it. During the year's camp meeting, he comes around after the general Choir practice, to assist me with my musical instrument. My friends constantly made jokes about how we would end up together to which I always have the response 'He is not my type' He is too good to be MINE. I never knew God had His plans. We stopped talking after the camp meeting.

On October 31, 2019, I was moved to upload a teaching of Mrs. Ibukun Awosika on my Whatsapp status, few minutes after I saw his message. They say true love often finds you when you least expect it. I met my God - Sent Lover when I wasn't searching. With serious prayers and confirmation, I began to take him seriously. From that moment, we came chat buddies, friends, and now Lovers.

Looking back, all I can see is God's hand in every single thing!

This love story doesn't end here; it is just the beginning of a lifetime commitment to each other. Join us as we celebrate this decision in the eyes of God and our family and loved ones.
